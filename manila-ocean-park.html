Manila Ocean Park

An oceanarium adventure showcasing the rich marine biodiversity of the Philippines

About Manila Ocean Park

Opened in 2008, Manila Ocean Park is an integrated urban oceanarium and marine-themed park located behind the Quirino Grandstand in Manila. The park features an impressive main oceanarium with a 25-meter walkway tunnel where visitors can observe marine creatures swimming overhead.

The park has expanded to include numerous attractions beyond the oceanarium, making it a full-day family destination with educational and entertaining experiences.

Main Attractions

  • Oceanarium - Home to 14,000 marine creatures from 277 species
  • Sea Lion Show - Entertaining and educational performances
  • Trails to Antarctica - Penguin encounter experience
  • Sharks and Rays Dry Encounter - Unique interactive exhibit
  • Jellies Exhibit - Hypnotic display of various jellyfish species
  • Birds of the Prey Kingdom - Raptor show featuring eagles and owls

Visitor Information

Location: Behind Quirino Grandstand, Rizal Park, Manila

Opening Hours: 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily (last entry at 7:00 PM)

Entrance Fees:
- Oceanarium Only: 750 PHP (adults), 600 PHP (children)
- All-Access Pass: 1,200 PHP (adults), 900 PHP (children)

Show Schedules: Vary by day - check website for current timetable

Dining Options: Several restaurants and cafes on site
